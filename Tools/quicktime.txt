FAQ
|- quicktime can not be opened
1. Situation1:
   Firewall disables quicktime. 
   Solution:
   Disable firewall.
   
2. It could be that the window of quicktime is not open but process is running. 
   Check it in windows task manager.
   If it's, kill the process and try again, then it could be opened in windows.
   Reason is not known.
   
3. If above two are not the reasons, it may be caused by it's related process C:\Program Files\Bonjour\mDNSResponder.exe.
   Double click to run mDNSResponder.exe first,which is a background running process, and try again.
